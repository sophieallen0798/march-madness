Frequently Asked Questions

I don't know anything about sports. Why are you trying to get me to do this?

It's fun! Amount of sports knowledge does not coorelate with amount of fun. Meet someone new and enjoy a low stakes game.

No one in history has gotten all of the games correct. There's a 1 in 9.2 quintillion chance. So it's really not a big deal to make a bad bracket.

What do I do?

Just go to Submit Bracket and click on a team for each game going all the way up the bracket until you choose a champion.

How to fill out your bracket

However you want. You can choose your favorite logos, compare AI models, copy a celebrity bracket, choose the teams you think will win, pick against your least favorite states, etc.

Scoring System:
  • Round 1: 1 point per correct pick
  • Round 2: 2 points
  • Round 3 (Sweet 16): 4 points
  • Round 4 (Elite Eight): 8 points
  • Round 5 (Final Four): 16 points
  • Championship: 32 points
What do I win?

Buy-in participants get to choose a charity to donate the funds to. If we have a volunteer with a 3D printer, participants that finish in the top 3 will get a 3D printed trophy.

Is it free?

Yes!

The buy-in is completely optional. Buy-in money is donated to a charity of the winner's choice.

How does the optional buy-in work?

The overall buy-in winner (men's bracket score + women's bracket score) gets to choose the charity for 50% of the pot. Buy-in winner of the men's and women's individual brackets split the remaining 50%. If the overall winner also wins one of the individual brackets, the 25% will go to second place in that category.

How do I fill out a winning bracket?

Seeds: Teams are ranked based on their performance in the regular season. 1 seeds are the best, 16 seeds are the worst.

Upsets: When a lower seed (like 16) beats a higher seed (like 1), it's refered to as an upset. Upsets are pretty common in Men's March Madness. There are usually only a couple a year in Women's March Madness.

If you want to get into it, you'll find a lot with a quick Google search.

Scores and standings are updated every 5 minutes.
